Mantra's Chefs prepare an array of vegetarian, vegan, chicken, lamb & seafood dishes that hail primarily from Northern India.

We use finest Indian ingredients & the freshest local produce to craft our Menu which includes our Signature Dishes — Paneer Butter Masala, Chicken Tikka Masala, Chicken Saag, Lamb Vindaloo, Goat Curry & Shrimp Tikka Masala to name a few.

Come and indulge yourself with the Best Indian Food in the Valley of the Sun. Join us for a quick lunch meal or an unforgettable evening to celebrate your special occasion!
